Getting Started

How does The Gastronomy Project work? +

It starts with a short wellness profile — just a few questions about your lifestyle, goals, and preferences. Based on your answers, a personalized formula is custom-formulated and shipped directly to your door. The entire process takes about three minutes.

How long does it take to get started? +

The online visit takes about three minutes. After your profile is reviewed, your formula is prepared and shipped — most customers receive their order within a few days.

Is there a commitment or contract? +

No commitment and no contract. You can adjust, pause, or cancel at any time. We want you to stay because it works for you — not because of fine print.

How much does it cost? +

Options start as low as $25 per month depending on the plan that's right for you. You'll see the full pricing breakdown before you finalize — no hidden fees, no surprises.

About Our Formulas

What makes your formulas different from what I'd buy in a store? +

Our approach features combinations including natural ingredients like ginger or mastic gum, prepared by our fulfillment partner and offered through a science backed process. Different from what's typically available elsewhere.

What ingredients do you use? +

Our formulas feature science-backed ingredients like ginger, mastic gum, and calcium carbonate. Every ingredient is selected with intention and supported by published research. You can view a full breakdown on our formulas page.

Is everything in one capsule? +

Yes. Your custom formula is combined into a single all-in-one capsule. No juggling bottles, no complicated routines — just one capsule designed around your daily life.
