Every ingredient is selected with intention. Here's what goes into your Weekend Formula capsule and the science behind it.

Ginger Root Extract
200mg per capsule

Sourced for potency and purity. Ginger has been used for centuries across cultures to support overall wellness. Our extract is standardized to deliver consistent levels of its key natural compounds.

Naturally contains
Gingerols Shogaols Zingerone Paradols

Why ginger? Ginger root is one of the most well studied natural wellness ingredients in the world. Its bioactive compounds, particularly gingerols and shogaols, are recognized for supporting everyday comfort. We chose concentrated Ginger Root Extract as the foundation of this formula because of its fast-acting profile and long history of safe, effective use.
