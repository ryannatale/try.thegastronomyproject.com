What Are Cookies?

A cookie is a small piece of data sent from a Website and stored on your device by your browser.

Our Placement and Classification of Cookies

This Website sets the cookies identified below. We have classified the cookies that we set by the purpose that they serve.

Functional Cookies

Functional cookies are cookies that allow a Website to remember the choices that you have made in the past and how you use the Website. For example, these cookies may be used for remembering what language you prefer, which pages you clicked on, or what your username and password are so that you can automatically log in to your account. Marketing cookies are cookies that track your online activity to, for example, help advertisers deliver more relevant advertisements or to limit how many times you see an advertisement. This Website does not collect any marketing cookies.
