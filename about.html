About Us

Modern Wellness, Rooted in Science

The Gastronomy Project was founded on a simple idea: personalized wellness should be accessible, transparent, and built around the way you actually live. No guesswork. No one-size-fits-all. Just thoughtful formulas, delivered to your door.

Why We Exist

For too long, wellness has been generic. Walk into any store and you'll find the same off-the-shelf options — formulas designed for everyone and optimized for no one. We believed there was a better way.

The Gastronomy Project brings together modern science, personalized profiling, and a licensed fulfillment partner to create custom-formulated capsules tailored to your unique needs. Every formula is built after a profile review, so what you receive is designed specifically for you.

We're not here to replace your existing wellness routine. We're here to make it smarter, more personal, and more convenient — all in one capsule, shipped directly to your door.

Ryan Natale
Founder, The Gastronomy Project

Ryan Natale founded The Gastronomy Project after years of seeing the same problem: people struggling to find wellness solutions that were actually built for them. Too many options were generic, confusing, or required juggling multiple products just to cover the basics.

His vision was straightforward — combine evidence-based formulation with a personalized approach, then deliver everything in a single capsule. No complexity. No confusion. Just a smarter way to support your everyday comfort.

Today, The Gastronomy Project partners with a licensed fulfillment partner to custom-formulate every order based on individual profiles. Each formula is reviewed, prepared, and shipped — so every customer receives exactly what was designed for them.
